KYC Verification Process

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is required to activate full account features, including withdrawals and high-value transactions.

Users must submit:
- Government-issued ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, or Driving License)
- A clear selfie for identity verification
- Address proof (if required)

Common reasons for KYC rejection:
- Blurry or cropped documents
- Mismatch between name on ID and account
- Expired identification documents
- Low-quality images or glare
